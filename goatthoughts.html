Overview

Goat Thoughts is a tool that allows people attending therapy, doctors, or other types of appointments to log and organize their thoughts and concerns inbetween sessions.

Simply add a provider, add an upcoming session, and start logging your Goat Thoughts for the next time you see your provider.

The Problem

Talking to professionals is intimidating for many people. Those who attend therapy appointments often forget what they want to talk to their provider about by the time the appointment starts

Therapy isn't just therapy

If your mind goes blank when you show up to your doctors' office, don't worry, you're not alone. This is a common response for many people in a variety of situations, but especially in a theraputic setting. When there's so much to get to, how do you know what to prioritize?
